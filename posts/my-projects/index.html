My Projects

Posted on Nov 26, 2024

I can say that I’m a serial maker and entrepreneur by spirit. From my early days on the Internet I try to create various online projects and convert them into stable businesses. When I do work for someone else (freelance, contractor jobs) I can’t say that every time I do the most interesting things out there, and this is where my ventures help me to keep going: I can learn whatever technology I want, I can do whatever I want in a way I want :) So it benefits a lot.

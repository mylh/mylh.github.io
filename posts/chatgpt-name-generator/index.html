ChatGPT Name Generator

Supercharge Your Startup Naming Process with ChatGPT & Domain Availability Checker

As indie hackers and entrepreneurs, we all know that coming up with the perfect name for our startups can be a daunting task. We’ve all been there, spending days or even weeks brainstorming names and searching for available domain names. But what if I told you that you can now generate dozens of creative names and find available domains in just minutes? Sounds too good to be true, right? Well, not anymore! Introducing the Startup Name Generator with ChatGPT and Domain Availability Checker, a utility I built to make this process easier and faster than ever before.

The utility leverages the power of ChatGPT. ChatGPT has the incredible ability to understand context and generate human-like text, making it the perfect tool for generating creative and unique startup name ideas based on a given description. With just a few lines of code, you can prompt ChatGPT to provide a list of potential startup names that are not only interesting but also memorable.

Once you have your list of potential names, the next step is to ensure the corresponding domain names are available for registration. This tool checks the availability of domain names for the generated startup names, saving you the time and effort of manually searching for domains.

To use the utility, simply provide a description of your startup, and the tool will generate a list of potential startup names. Next, it will check the availability of the corresponding domain names and display the results in the terminal. With this utility, you can now get dozens of good names and domains in just a matter of minutes!

The utility is open source and available on GitHub. You can easily set it up on your local machine and start generating names for your projects right away. Installation is straightforward, and the utility is designed to be user-friendly: https://github.com/mylh/chatgpt-name-generator

In today’s fast-paced world of entrepreneurship, time is of the essence. Spending countless hours brainstorming names and searching for available domains is no longer a productive use of your time. With the Startup Name Generator with ChatGPT and Domain Availability Checker, you can now supercharge your naming process and focus on what really matters: building and growing your startup.

So, fellow indie hackers, give this utility a try and let me know your thoughts. I’m confident it will help streamline your naming process and bring you one step closer to launching your next successful venture.

Happy hacking!
